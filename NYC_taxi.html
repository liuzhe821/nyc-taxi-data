NYC Taxi Map

This is an interactive visualization tool for the yellow taxi flows in New York City in May 2016 — by location and by time. The map is divided into "Neighborhood Tabulation Areas" (NTAs) and the colors show density of pickups, dropoffs, their difference, etc. The data is from the NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission (TLC). © 2017 Victoria Lin, Zhe Liu, Costis Maglaras, Aruna Prasad, Georgina Davis, Cyndia Yu
